Recycling Tips

Recycle!

♻️ How to Recycle

  • Sort your trash — paper, plastic, glass & metal go in separate bins
  • Use reusable bags instead of single-use plastic bags
  • Donate old clothes and toys instead of throwing them away
  • Turn old jars into pencil holders or plant pots
  • Use BOTH sides of paper before recycling it
  • Compost fruit peels to make natural fertiliser for plants!
💧

Save Water Ideas

💧 Easy Ways to Save Water

  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth — saves 8 litres!
  • Take shorter showers — 5 minutes is enough!
  • Fix dripping taps — one drip wastes thousands of litres a year!
  • Water plants in the morning or evening, not at midday
  • Collect rainwater to water your garden
  • Use a bucket instead of a hose when washing the car

Pollution Facts You Should Know

🌬️

Air Pollution

Over 7 million people get sick every year because of air pollution from cars and factories!

🌊

Ocean Pollution

8 million tonnes of plastic enter our oceans every year — sea turtles and dolphins are badly hurt!

🌱

Soil Pollution

Factory chemicals poison the ground and damage the crops that we eat every day!

How Kids Can Help the Planet!

🏫 At School

  • Switch off lights when leaving the classroom
  • Use both sides of paper for notes and drawings
  • Start a recycling corner in your class!
  • Plant trees or flowers in the school garden
  • Walk or cycle to school when possible

🏠 At Home

  • Remind your family to recycle and not litter
  • Unplug chargers when not in use
  • Choose a reusable water bottle
  • Eat more veggies and fruits — they help the planet!
  • Teach younger siblings about protecting Earth
